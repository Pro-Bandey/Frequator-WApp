Frequator WApp

I made an tone generator Frequator WApp using the HTML5 Web Audio API. It provides smooth, real-time logarithmic frequency changes.

Features

  • Fine-tune frequency in 1 Hz, 0.01 Hz and 0.001 Hz steps
  • Choose musical notes (revamped May 2016)
  • Shift frequency by a full octave
  • Smooth slider movement
  • Keyboard shortcuts
  • Shareable tone links
  • Waveform selection: sine, square, sawtooth, triangle
  • Direct numeric frequency input
  • Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, mobile

Other tone generators exist, but many rely on Java or Flash — mine is lightweight, clean, and works directly in the browser.

What can you use a tone generator for?

Experiment with resonance, tune instruments, test audio systems, check hearing limits, or identify your tinnitus frequency for therapy.
